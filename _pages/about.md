I am an Assistant Professor at the [Escola Politécnica (Engineering School)](https://www.poli.usp.br/) of the [University of São Paulo (USP)](https://www5.usp.br/#english) 🇧🇷.
I was Postdoctoral Researcher at the [Artificial Intelligence and Cognitive Architectures Hub (H.IAAC)](https://hiaac.unicamp.br/) from 2023 to 2024. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the [Institute of Computing (IC)](https://ic.unicamp.br/en/) from UNICAMP in September 2023. In 2018, I was awarded a prestigious doctoral scholarship from the [São Paulo Research Foundation (FAPESP)](https://fapesp.br/).

During the doctorate, I spent one year (Research Internships Abroad - FAPESP) as a visiting researcher with the [Networked Systems Modeling](https://www.cms-labs.org/) research group of Professor [Christoph Sommer](https://www.cms-labs.org/people/sommer/) in the Faculty of Computer Science at [Technische Universität Dresden (TU Dresden)](https://tu-dresden.de/ing/informatik), Germany 🇩🇪, from March 2022 to March 2023.

Before joining UNICAMP, I earned a BS in Information Systems (2015) and an MS in Computer Science (2018), both from the [Federal University of Pará (UFPA)][2].

I am a researcher, investigating complex problems related to:

* Vehicular Edge Computing (VEC) and Vehicular Clouds (VCs)
* Task Scheduling & Resource Allocation
* Distributed Learning
* Open Radio Access Networks (starting research on this topic...)

See more in the [detailed CV](http://lattes.cnpq.br/4761632587625158).
